ScoreLog - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

ScoreLog ("the App") is developed by Bramzz. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use the App.

Information We Collect

ScoreLog does not collect any personal information directly. All game data (scores, player names, game history) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

However, the App uses third-party services that may collect certain information:

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data including your advertising ID, device information, and general location to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

  • Google Privacy Policy

Firebase Crashlytics

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic data. This helps us identify and fix bugs. Data collected includes device type, operating system version, and crash logs. This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

  • Firebase Privacy Policy

Google Play In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional in-app purchase to remove advertisements. Purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Advertising ID

The App requests access to your device's advertising ID (AD_ID) for the purpose of serving ads through Google AdMob. You can reset or disable your advertising ID through your Android device settings under Privacy > Ads.

Android Permissions & Background Services

On Android, the App requests the following permissions:

  • Foreground Service (Media Playback) — The App uses a foreground service of type mediaPlayback to play sound effects via the expo-audio library. This service runs only while audio is active and does not collect or transmit any data.
  • Vibrate — Used for haptic feedback on button presses and score updates.
  • Internet — Required to load advertisements and submit crash reports.

Data Storage

All app data including scores, player names, game settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device. This data is not backed up to any external server. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The ads shown are configured to comply with applicable regulations.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
