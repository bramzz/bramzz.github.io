Wrecked - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Wrecked ("the App") is developed by BDG Apps. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use the App.

Information We Collect

Wrecked does not collect any personal information. All game data (player names, scores, game settings) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Google Play In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to unlock premium content (additional game modes and vibes). Purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information.

  • Google Privacy Policy

No Ads, No Analytics

Wrecked does not display advertisements and does not use any analytics or crash reporting services. No data is collected or transmitted from the App.

Android Permissions

On Android, the App requests the following permissions:

  • Vibrate — Used for haptic feedback during gameplay.
  • Internet — Required to process in-app purchases through Google Play.
  • Billing — Required to communicate with Google Play Billing for in-app purchases.

Data Storage

All app data including player names, game progress, and preferences are stored locally on your device. This data is not backed up to any external server. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Wrecked is a drinking game intended for users of legal drinking age. It is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
